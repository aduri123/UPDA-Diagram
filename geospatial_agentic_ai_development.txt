          <li>LLM + RAG (policy docs with citations).</li>
          <li>Knowledge Graph (openCypher) for rule paths & relationships.</li>
          <li>Tool-calling for spatial validation & queries.</li>
        </ul>
        <pre class="mt-3 bg-slate-50 border border-slate-200 rounded-xl p-3 text-sm overflow-auto">MATCH (p:Parcel)-[:LOCATED_IN]->(z:Zone)

          <li>Feature store by <code>entity_id</code> (parcel_id, asset_id, case_id).</li>
        </ul>
